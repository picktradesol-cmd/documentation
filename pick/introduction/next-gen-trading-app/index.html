Next-gen Trading App

Pick Trade is revolutionizing the Solana ecosystem by functioning as far more than a traditional trading app. It is a comprehensive hub for launching, trading, analyzing, and socializing.

By integrating functionalities that are usually isolated across different applications such as minting (PumpFun), trading (Axiom), or analyzing (DexScreener), Pick Trade offers a unified suite designed for speed, convenience, and efficiency.

Trading Platform & Token Screener

Pick Trade establishes itself as one of the fastest trading platform built on Solana, executing transactions in less than a second.

  • Bot-Level Features: The platform offers an intuitive interface with advanced features similar to trading bots.
  • One-Click Trading: Users can buy and sell with a single click. By eliminating the need to sign messages for every transaction, the process is streamlined for exceptional speed.
  • Advanced Analysis: Acting as a powerful token screener, Pick Trade provides sophisticated charts, tools, and detailed data, allowing users to evaluate any token on the market with the depth of dedicated analysis platforms.

Launchpad Aggregator

Pick Trade operates as a premier launchpad aggregator. This unique structure empowers token creators to deploy their assets on any existing launchpad built on Solana while managing it through one interface. Thus benefiting from creator rewards from both the underlying launchpad and Pick.trade.

Beyond deployment, Pick Trade offers advanced customization: creators can easily configure reflection mechanics to automatically reward holders or implement deflationary models to induce scarcity.

Furthermore, the creator suite provides professional post-launch support, connecting projects with market makers, marketing agencies, KOL and more.

On-chain Social Network

Pick Trade introduces a social layer that transforms solitary trading into a community experience. Unlike competitors restricted to specific niches, any token on Solana can benefit from these social features.

Users can engage in real-time through dedicated chat rooms on any tokens page and participate in interactive live video streams, fostering a lively environment around every asset.

This social foundation is designed to support future developments across all asset classes, extending well beyond standard crypto. For instance, through the integration of xStocks, Pick Trade stands as the only platform where users can trade on-chain stocks while earning incentives and interacting with fellow traders.

Gamified dApp Experience

To drive engagement, the platform is fully gamified. Every user possesses a unique avatar with customizable accessories that reflect their status.

Most interactions on the platform contribute to a user's accumulation of XP. As traders level up, they unlock exclusive rewards. Furthermore, over 50 quests are available, offering unlockable badges to showcase achievements within the community.
