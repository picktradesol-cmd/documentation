Gamified Trading

While Pick Trade has the ambition to become a global trading platform, one of the main focuses is to gamify the trading experience. Trading assets onchain and interacting with smart contracts need to be easier, more fun and more attractive. The approach must be the same as how video games are seen today.

Customization

Several aspects of the platform are customizable, starting with the profile section. Each user can create and personalize their own avatar with a wide variety of accessories. While some items are available immediately, others must be earned.

Just like in video games, users need to earn XP and advance through levels to unlock these exclusive assets. This is done by actively engaging with the platform and completing various achievements.

Earning

A XP-earning system based on on-chain interactions has been implemented, featuring a total of 100 levels. For each transaction executed on Pick Trade, users earn XP which is added to their profiles. At specific milestones, users advance to higher levels, with each subsequent level requiring more XP to complete. As users level up, new perks become unlockable.

Currently, three types of rewards are distributed upon leveling up:

  • Cashback: Users start with 20% cashback, which increases as they level up, reaching a maximum of 35% at Level 100.
  • Avatars: Exclusive avatar skins remain locked until specific levels are reached.
  • $PICK: $PICK tokens are distributed based on the volume of the weekly buyback executed by the team.

Quests & Badges

Pick Trade has also introduced trading badges, which are earned by unlocking specific achievements. These achievements are varied and based on both on-chain and off-chain tasks, such as inviting 10 friends or completing 50 swaps on the platform.

A total of 152 badges have been implemented, each with a distinct rarity rank. Users can proudly display these badges on their profiles, allowing anyone to view their achievements.

Leaderboard

On top of that comes the leaderboard, a general scoreboard ranking users based on their total point earnings. Points can be earned by completing quests, unlocking badges, reaching new levels or even by simply connecting to the platform daily. The points system is detailed on the leaderboard page.

This leaderboard is then utilized by the Pick Trade team to allocate airdrops, distribute rewards, and grant exclusive perks.

Reward Center

No gamified platform is complete without a dedicated space for users to track and claim their success. The Pick Trade reward center is an interactive hub where users can monitor their progress through levels and achievements, as well as claim their earned rewards.

A total of four financial rewards are available:

  • Cashback: Earn up to 35% cashback depending on your trading volume on the platform.
  • Referral: Earn up to 40% lifetime commission on every user you refer.
  • Pick Trade Creator Rewards: Token creators earn 20% of the fees from every swap on their token.
  • Launchpad Creator Rewards: Claim rewards generated by the underlying native launchpad directly on Pick.trade (such as the pumpfun creator rewards).

Integrating incentives and gamification into a trading platform is crucial. Therefore, we will continue to expand these features and focus on making the trading experience more enjoyable for users.
